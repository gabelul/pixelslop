The Chef

Luca Ferretti

Executive Chef & Co-Founder

Luca grew up in his grandmother's kitchen in the hills outside Siena, where cooking wasn't a career — it was how you showed love. After a decade staging through Michelin kitchens in Lyon and Copenhagen, he came back to what he knew: simple ingredients, live fire, zero waste.

"The best dish I ever made was the one my nonna would've nodded at. No foam, no tweezers. Just food that makes you close your eyes."
